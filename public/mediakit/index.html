Elevator Pitch

PVD Snow makes it easy to report unplowed streets and unshoveled sidewalks in Providence. Pick the issue, snap a photo, confirm the address — done in 30 seconds. We forward every report directly to the city's official 311 system.

It's a volunteer project, not affiliated with the city. Just trying to make it easier for people to report problems and hold the city accountable after storms.

Key Facts

  • What: A mobile web app (PWA) for reporting snow-related issues in Providence, RI
  • How it works: Reports are forwarded to the city's official 311 system on behalf of the user
  • Why: The city's 311 portal is a multi-step desktop form — painful to use on a phone while standing in a snowbank
  • Who: Built by a Providence resident, entirely volunteer-run
  • Cost: Free. No account required. No personal information collected unless you choose to add it.
  • URL: pvdsnow.org
