## Subject line

Providence resident builds free app to simplify snow reporting after historic storm

## Tip

After this weekend's 20-27 inch snowfall, a Providence resident has launched pvdsnow.org — a free app that lets people file a 311 report for unplowed streets or unshoveled sidewalks in about 30 seconds from their phone.

Reports go directly to the city's official 311 system. The app is designed to make it easy for residents to file 311 reports and help the city see where snow removal is still needed.

The project is volunteer-run and not affiliated with the City of Providence.
